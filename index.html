About

This story map is meant to illustrate my time in Senegal as a Peace Corps Volunteer from 2014-2016. It was made as part of a project for Professor Bo Zhao at Oregon State University as part of the class GEOG371 Geovisualization. Its functionality relies on both leaflet and Boostrap.

storymap.js | Released Date: 02/20/2017 | Version: 2.1 | MIT License

Author: Grant Zoch | College of Earth, Ocean, Atmospheric Science | Oregon State University

The background video on the front page was clipped from Youtube, produced by CNN.

The favicon image was acquired from http://www.udlcenter.org/advocacy/state/oregon.

This story map uses geospatial data from mapbox, Google Earth Engine, ESRI Map, Open Street Maps and github.
